Welcome!

Hello! My name is Chandler White, and I'm a GIS professional with over 15 years of experience in both the public and private sectors. I currently serve as the GIS Technical Coordinator with the Department of Information Technology at Clayton County, Georgia.

My professional interests lie in spatial analysis, ecological theory and research, and application development, while my skills range from enterprise system design to field data collection. I'm passionate about applying geospatial technology to address real-world challenges and support data-driven decision making.
